My Dearest Love

Duniya ke shor-gul se pare, tum meri khamosh panaah ho—jahan mera dil bas sukoon se basera karta hai.

Tumhari hansi woh narm sa sur hai jo mere dinon ko geet, aur raaton ko dua bana deti hai.

Tumhari aankhon mein sirf haseeniyat nahi; meri har khwahish ko manzil tak pahunchaane ka hausla chhupa hai.

Bas tum—tumhari meharbaani, tumhari nazakat, tumhara pyaar—aur duniya pal bhar mein aur bhi noorani ho jaati hai.

Har din tumhare saath ek nayi kahani phoolti hai; kal ki subah bhi jaise hamare naam se roshni leti hai.

Tum meri sabse badi barkat, meri roshni, mera pyaar ho—aaj, kal, aur hamesha.
